## Content Rules (Critical)

Read [references/content-rules.md](references/content-rules.md) for the full content strategy ruleset. The non-negotiables:

1. **Launch framing** — Build authority, don't claim it. "Here's what I'm building" not "I've helped hundreds."
2. **Storytelling first** — Every piece of content tells a story. No listicles, no lectures.
3. **Faith naturally** — Bible verse that connects to the topic. Not forced. Not preachy.
4. **beSettld is NOT real estate** — It's luxury in-home concierge. No "first-time buyer" content.
5. **Make him want to film** — Scripts should be exciting and feel like something he'd actually say.
6. **Cross-venture isolation** — Each venture has its own audience and voice. Don't cross-contaminate in the content itself. The ventures discover each other through the person, not through the content.
7. **Props that argue** — Every prop must reveal, simplify, contrast, prove, or anchor. Never decorate.
8. **The skeptic gets dignity** — Never straw-man counterarguments. Give them full marks where correct, then show where they have less ground.
9. **Verse capstone is load-bearing** — Arrives last, quietly, reframes everything without announcing itself as religious content.
